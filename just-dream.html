Just Dream

Just Dream is a narrative and puzzles game which tells the story of a kid whose biggest dream is to be an astronaut developed with Unity3D.

Live Link

Project Overview

Just Dream was the second game I developed and the first one I worked on during my university years, as part of a course in the Game Design and Development Bacherlor's Degree at ESNE-UDIT.

In this project, I took on the roles of producer, designer, and programmer, facing for the first time a more ambitious development that involved a team of six people. It was a key experience that taught me how to coordinate a multidisciplinary team and manage a project with greater demands.

Under the theme "The Journey to the Moon", our goal was to develop a game in Unity3D and publish it on the Play Store. This is how Just Dream was born—a mobile game for Android in which players take control of a young boy whose biggest dream is to become an astronaut and travel to the Moon.

One afternoon, feeling bored, he begins to imagine that his house is a rocket he can pilot to reach his destination. However, during his journey, unexpected problems arise, and he must repair his ship to make his dream come true.

The game was, unfortunately, removed from the Play Store due to lack of updates to new Android versions. However, it can still be found in "other" stores.
