Meet Nicolás

Professional Profile

With over five years of professional experience as Unity and .NET developer, Nicolás has worked on a variety of projects, including architectural and engineering tools, BIM visualization tools for Revit, Unity-integrated tools, and game development (first-person, puzzle, and strategy games), as well as software architecture.

Beyond development, he has led small multidisciplinary teams, managing programmers, designers, and artists, as well as mentoring university interns. Nicolás has experience in agile methodologies (Scrum), waterfall development, and TDD.

In addition to his professional development work, he is also a lecturer in Artificial Intelligence for Game Design and Development Bachelor's Degree at UDIT.
