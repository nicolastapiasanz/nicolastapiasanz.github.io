Warren Of Love

Ludum Dare 41 / Gamejamon 2018

Warren Of Love is a shooter and resource management game with gore and humor elements in which the player has to manage a small rabbit warren ruled by a terrible queen rabbit. The player has to cultivate carrots, make them reproduce with the queen rabbit and liquidate them to generate fertilizer for the carrots.

It was a game that was developed over three days for Ludum Dare 41. I played functions of programmer and designer.

It was developed in collaboration with: Gonzalo Pérez, as programmer; Nuria Calzada, as 2D Artist; and Juan Fernandez, as 2D Artist and Animator.

We won Best Audio Award at ESNE Gamejamon 2018.
