Emergency Room: Dr. Mortimer on Call!

Unknown Outcome

Emergency Room: Dr. Mortimer on Call! is a 3D overhead game in which the player controls a doctor and a nurse in his daily routine of saving lives. It is an arcade and multiplayer experience with a progressive complexity and a playful aesthetic. Developed in Unity Engine with C#.

In this project I had several positions. My main job was Producer of the Unknown Outcome university team. In this position, I was in charge of supervising the development, controlling the times of each member, organizing, schematizing and assigning each task and writing all this information weeek after week. We work thorugh the Scrum methodology adapted to our position as university students.

In this project I also worked as gameplay and mechanic designer. I was in charge of writing all the necessary documentation so that the artist and programmers could work, detailing all the elements of the scene, the interaction with it as well as gameflow.

On the other hand, I worked as gameplay, mechanics, controller and UI programmer. I worked as the main programmer of the team developing all the main logic of the game. The development was in Unity Engine with C#.

In this project, worked with me: Alberto Guzmán, as 3D Generalist and Technical Artist; Pablo Trascasa, as Gameplay Programmer; Steven Kormanik, as Game Designer; and Daniel de Vicente, as Concept Artist.
