Just Dream

Nucelar Studios

Just Dream is an small narative game for Android which tells the story of a kid whose biggest dream is to be an astronaut. With his imagination, everything is possible and begins to transform all his house into a rocket which travels to the moon. In this mission, he is the commander. But in his trip, the rocket has problems and, suddenly, it runs out of energy. To achieve his dreams, the player must help him solving several puzzles in order to fix the machine.
