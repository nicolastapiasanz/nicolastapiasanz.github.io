M. The City in the Center of the World

Gammera Nest

M.The City in the Center of The World (or Nubla 2) is a 2D adventure that tells the story of the search of a city that lies beyond dreams. It is a game whithin the PlayStation Talents framework published exclusively on PS4 in collaboration with the Thyssen-Bornemisza museum.

For a summer, I was collaborating in the design department, organizing and developing characters, helping with the game script and gameplay design
