Simulation of Masses


This was an investigation project I did in my last year in my University, ESNE in collaboration with Lenovo and the Madrid City council. The objective was to create a simulation of the Puerta del Sol, one of the most important squares in Spain, to observe the behavior of population masses. In my case, I was working with my partner Gonzalo Perez to design the program, but I wrote the code. We decide to make the simulation in Unity with C# because it would be more easies to have control of the simulation and its optimization. All the art of the scene was create by a group of students of ESTEC but I made some improvements in the illumination and shadows to make the simulation more efficient. The create enough assets to represent all the scene a one of them set the scene.

I was working with the NavMesh Unity provides but, because we had three types of scenes (one for normal situations, another for Christmas and anothe for a Champions League event) I had to bake the NavMesh in the three scenes.

Because the Puerta del Sol square is really big, it is necessary to have the possibility to have thousands and thousands of people moving around the sqare, going shooping, looking for spectacles, etc. I had to develop two different cases: one with capsules instead of 3D real characters to have more efficient and with more realistic data; and other one with 3D characters to show the realism of the scene.

The project is still in progress, but I am not working on it right now.
